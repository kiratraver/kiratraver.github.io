MORT WALKER: COMIC ILLUSTRATOR


    Creator of “Beetle Bailey” and co-creator of “Hi and Lois” and “Sam and Silo”
    Comics in over 1,800 newspapers, 50 countries, 200 million readers daily
    Founder of the National Cartoon Museum (1974-2002)
    1989 inducted into Cartoon Art Hall of Fame
