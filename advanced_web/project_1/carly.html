CARLY SIMON: MUSICIAN


    Singer, songwriter, author
    Grammy for Best New Artist for “That’s the Way I’ve Always Heard It To Be”
    Three Grammys for “You’re So Vain”
    Grammy for “Coming Around Again”
    The first artist in history to win all three major awards (Grammy, Academy, and Golden Globe) for “Let the River Run”
